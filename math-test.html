Rotation of a vector around a unit vector

$$ \vec{a} = \begin{bmatrix} a_x \\ a_y \\ a_z \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ \rvert \vec{a} \rvert = \sqrt{a_x^2 + a_y^2 + a_z^2} = 1 $$ $$ a_x^2 + a_y^2 + a_z^2 = 1 \text{, } - a_y^2 + a_z^2 = a_x^2 - 1 \text{, } - a_x^2 + a_z^2 = a_y^2 - 1 \text{, } - a_x^2 + a_y^2 = a_z^2 - 1 $$ $$ \mathbb{I} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ \mathbb{S} = \begin{bmatrix} \vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times} = \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0\phantom{_x} & -a_z & \phantom{+}a_y \\ \phantom{+}a_z & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_y} & -a_x \\ -a_y & \phantom{+}a_x & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_z} \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ \mathbb{S}^T = \begin{bmatrix} \vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times}^T = \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0\phantom{_x} & \phantom{+}a_z & -a_y \\ -a_z & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_y} & \phantom{+}a_x \\ \phantom{+}a_y & -a_x & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_z} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -\vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times} = -\mathbb{S} $$ $$ \mathbb{S}^2 = \left ( \begin{bmatrix} \vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times} \right )^{2} \\ = \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0 & -a_z & \phantom{+}a_y \\ \phantom{+}a_z & \phantom{+}0 & -a_x \\ -a_y & \phantom{+}a_x & \phantom{+}0 \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0 & -a_z & \phantom{+}a_y \\ \phantom{+}a_z & \phantom{+}0 & -a_x \\ -a_y & \phantom{+}a_x & \phantom{+}0 \end{bmatrix} \\ = \begin{bmatrix} -(a_y^2 + a_z^2) & a_y \cdot a_x & a_z \cdot a_x \\ a_x \cdot a_y & -(a_x^2 + a_z^2) & a_z \cdot a_y \\ a_x \cdot a_z & a_y \cdot a_z & -(a_x^2 + a_y^2) \end{bmatrix} \\ = \begin{bmatrix} a_x^2 - 1 & a_y \cdot a_x & a_z \cdot a_x \\ a_x \cdot a_y & a_y^2 - 1 & a_z \cdot a_y \\ a_x \cdot a_z & a_y \cdot a_z & a_z^2 - 1 \end{bmatrix} \\ = \begin{bmatrix} a_x \cdot a_x & a_y \cdot a_x & a_z \cdot a_x \\ a_x \cdot a_y & a_y \cdot a_y & a_z \cdot a_y \\ a_x \cdot a_z & a_y \cdot a_z & a_z \cdot a_z \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \\ = \begin{bmatrix} a_x \\ a_y \\ a_z \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} a_x \\ a_y \\ a_z \end{bmatrix}^{T} - \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \\ = \vec{a} \otimes \vec{a} - \mathbb{I} $$

Rodrigues' rotation formula

Rotation of a vector \( \vec{v} \) around the unit vector \( \vec{a} \) in a right handed coordinate system is \( \mathbb{R}_{rh} \cdot \vec{v} \), where $$ \mathbb{R}_{rh} = \mathbb{I} + \sin \alpha \cdot \mathbb{S} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \mathbb{S}^2 $$ $$ = \sin \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0\phantom{_x} & -a_z & \phantom{+}a_y \\ \phantom{+}a_z & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_y} & -a_x \\ -a_y & \phantom{+}a_x & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_z} \end{bmatrix} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} a_x \cdot a_x & a_y \cdot a_x & a_z \cdot a_x \\ a_x \cdot a_y & a_y \cdot a_y & a_z \cdot a_y \\ a_x \cdot a_z & a_y \cdot a_z & a_z \cdot a_z \end{bmatrix} + \cos \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ = \phantom{+}\sin \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} \vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \vec{a} \otimes \vec{a} + \cos \alpha \cdot \mathbb{I} $$
Rotation of a vector \( \vec{v} \) around the unit vector \( \vec{a} \) in a left handed coordinate system is \( \mathbb{R}_{lh} \cdot \vec{v} \), where $$ \mathbb{R}_{lh} = \mathbb{R}_{rh}^{-1} = \mathbb{R}_{rh}^T $$ $$ \mathbb{R}_{lh} = \mathbb{I} + \sin \alpha \cdot \mathbb{S}^T + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \mathbb{S}^2 = \mathbb{I} - \sin \alpha \cdot \mathbb{S} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \mathbb{S}^2 $$ $$ = \sin \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{+}0\phantom{_x} & \phantom{+}a_z & -a_y \\ -a_z & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_y} & \phantom{+}a_x \\ \phantom{+}a_y & -a_x & \phantom{+}0\phantom{_z} \end{bmatrix} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} a_x \cdot a_x & a_y \cdot a_x & a_z \cdot a_x \\ a_x \cdot a_y & a_y \cdot a_y & a_z \cdot a_y \\ a_x \cdot a_z & a_y \cdot a_z & a_z \cdot a_z \end{bmatrix} + \cos \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$ $$ = -\sin \alpha \cdot \begin{bmatrix} \vec{a} \end{bmatrix}_{\times} + (1 - \cos \alpha) \cdot \vec{a} \otimes \vec{a} + \cos \alpha \cdot \mathbb{I} $$
